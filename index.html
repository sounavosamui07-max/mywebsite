Best NEET Study Tips for Beginners

Start with NCERT books, make short notes, and revise daily. Focus more on Biology and practice MCQs regularly. Consistency is the key to cracking NEET.

How to Make a Perfect Study Timetable

Divide your day into study blocks of 2–3 hours. Include time for revision and breaks. Study difficult subjects when your mind is fresh.

Top Mistakes NEET Students Should Avoid

Avoid skipping NCERT, not revising regularly, and studying without practice. Do mock tests weekly and analyze your mistakes.
